We offer hypnotherapy, acupressure and reiki services to the Central Pennsylvania area

Hypnotherapy

Hypnotherapy is a form of psychotherapy used to create subconscious change in a patient in the form of new responses, thoughts, attitudes, behaviours or feelings.

Acupressure

Acupressure is based on the concept of life energy which flows through “meridians” in the body. Physical pressure is applied to acupuncture points with the aim of clearing blockages in these meridians. Pressure may be applied by hand, by elbow, or with various devices.

Reiki

Since originating in Japan, Reiki has been adapted into varying cultural traditions across the world. Reiki practitioners use a technique called palm healing or hands-on healing by which energy is transferred through the palms of the practitioner to a patient in order to encourage healing.
